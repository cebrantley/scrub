How to export your Instagram data

  1. Open Instagram → Settings & Privacy
  2. Tap Your Activity → Download Your Information
  3. Select Comments and Followers & Following, choose JSON format, and request download
  4. Wait for Instagram's email, then download the .zip file
  5. Drop the entire ZIP here — Scrub will find the comments automatically
🔒 100% private. Your file is analyzed entirely in your browser — nothing is ever uploaded anywhere.
